About
us

Founded in 1985, the Modern Art Institute has a storied history of championing modern art education. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between tradition and innovation in the art world.

Over the years, we have remained committed to our mission: to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration needed to thrive in the dynamic world of modern art, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and cultural enrichment.
